Welcome to Occupancy

The first fully decentralised platform to check the occupancy of restaurants and bars in Malaga.

The project

The Occupancy project is based on creatin a decentralized system where users can check, through a subscription system, the occupancy of restaurants and bars in Malaga, preserving their privacy and security using the new NuCypher network. To do this, follow the steps below:

Log in with the popular "Metamask" browser extension and link your Ethereum wallet.
